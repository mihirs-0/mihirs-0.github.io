Projects & Experiments

Reverse GPT (Arrow of Time)

I'm investigating reversal invariance in autoregressive language models—why the standard next-token objective is effectively direction-blind. I'm exploring Asymmetry-Aware Pretraining (AAP) as a way to give models a preference for forward causal flow.

"We identify an overlooked symmetry in autoregressive language modeling: reversal invariance—the standard next-token objective is indifferent to sequence direction."
"Asymmetry-Aware Pretraining (AAP) introduces regularization and evaluation that favor forward causal flow, encouraging models to encode an arrow of time."

Status: tokenizer experiments complete; training runs in progress; writing sample prepared for PhD apps.
